Frequently Asked Questions

What security certifications does TalentPrism hold?+
TalentPrism is SOC II Type 2 compliant and maintains GDPR compliance. We undergo regular third-party audits and security assessments to ensure the highest standards of data protection.
How is my data encrypted?+
All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. We use secure key management practices with regular key rotation to maintain optimal security.
Can I control who accesses candidate data?+
Yes, TalentPrism offers granular role-based access controls. You can define permissions at the user, team, and organizational level to ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive information.
What happens if there's a security breach?+
Our 24/7 security monitoring team responds immediately to any potential incidents. We follow established protocols for breach notification and work with affected customers to mitigate any impact.
