General Questions

What is TalentPrism?+
TalentPrism is an AI-powered recruitment platform that combines an intuitive Applicant Tracking System (ATS) with a comprehensive Recruitment CRM. We help companies streamline their hiring process, automate candidate screening, and find the right talent faster.
Who is TalentPrism designed for?+
TalentPrism is designed for recruitment agencies, staffing firms, executive search companies, freelance recruiters, and in-house HR teams of all sizes. Whether you're hiring for one position or managing hundreds of placements, our platform scales to your needs.
Do I need technical knowledge to use TalentPrism?+
No technical knowledge is required. TalentPrism is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. Our platform features a simple, modern interface that requires no steep learning curve. Plus, we provide comprehensive onboarding and training support.
Is my data secure with TalentPrism?+
Absolutely. We take data security very seriously. TalentPrism is SOC II Type 2 certified and fully GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we implement industry-leading security protocols to protect your information.

Features & Functionality

What features does TalentPrism offer?+
TalentPrism offers a comprehensive suite of features including: AI-powered candidate sourcing, candidate profile enrichment, applicant tracking system, AI recommendations for candidate matching, branded career pages, recruitment CRM, collaboration tools, data privacy compliance, security features, and integrations with 2,500+ job boards.
How does AI candidate enrichment work?+
Our AI engine automatically scans 20+ social media and public platforms (including LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, GitHub, and more) to enrich candidate profiles with additional information beyond their resume. This happens seamlessly during candidate creation, providing you with a complete view of each candidate.
Can I customize my recruitment pipeline?+
Yes! TalentPrism offers full customization of hiring stages, workflows, and permissions. You can adapt the platform to match your specific recruitment methodology using our intuitive drag-and-drop interface.
How many job boards can I post to?+
TalentPrism integrates with 2,500+ free and premium job boards globally, including Indeed, LinkedIn, Monster, CareerJet, JobStreet, and many more. You can post to multiple boards simultaneously and manage all sponsored job advertising campaigns from a single platform.
Is TalentPrism mobile-friendly?+
Yes! TalentPrism is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. You can access all features from your computer, phone, or tablet, allowing you to recruit on the go and never miss important activities.
Can I integrate TalentPrism with other tools?+
Absolutely. TalentPrism supports native integrations with job boards, communication tools, calendars, storage providers, and more. We also offer API access and webhooks for custom integrations. If you need a specific integration, contact us to discuss feasibility.

Security & Compliance

Is TalentPrism GDPR compliant?+
Yes. TalentPrism is fully GDPR compliant. We provide tools to help you manage candidate consent, data deletion requests, and privacy requirements. Learn more on our Data Privacy page.
What security certifications do you have?+
TalentPrism is SOC II Type 2 certified, demonstrating our commitment to the highest security standards. We undergo regular third-party audits to ensure ongoing compliance.
Where is my data stored?+
Your data is stored in secure, enterprise-grade data centers with redundancy and backup systems. All data is encrypted both in transit (using TLS/SSL) and at rest (using AES-256 encryption).
Can I export my data?+
Yes. You maintain full ownership of your data and can export it at any time in standard formats (CSV, Excel). There are no lock-in contracts or data hostage situations.
Do you offer SSO (Single Sign-On)?+
Yes. SAML SSO is available on our Enterprise plan, allowing you to integrate TalentPrism with your existing identity management system for secure, seamless authentication.
