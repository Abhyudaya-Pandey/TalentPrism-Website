Frequently Asked Questions

What is candidate enrichment?+
Candidate enrichment is the process of automatically gathering additional information about candidates from various online sources to create more comprehensive profiles, helping recruiters make better hiring decisions.
How does AI resume parsing work?+
Our AI-powered resume parsing uses large language models to automatically extract and classify candidate information from resumes, including skills, experience, and qualifications, into the appropriate database fields.
Can I import data from LinkedIn?+
Yes, our LinkedIn integration allows you to import candidate attributes directly from LinkedIn profiles, including experiences, skills, and contact information, using our Chrome extension.
Is my data secure?+
Yes, we maintain the highest security protocols, including SOC II Type 2 compliance, ensuring your candidate data is protected and stored securely in our cloud infrastructure.
