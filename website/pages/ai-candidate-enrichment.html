What makes TalentPrism's AI enrichment different?+
TalentPrism's enrichment goes beyond basic data collection by using advanced AI to analyze candidate profiles across 25+ platforms, identifying hidden skills, career patterns, and professional achievements that traditional methods miss.
How accurate is the AI resume parsing?+
Our AI parsing technology achieves 95%+ accuracy in extracting and categorizing candidate information, automatically populating profile fields with skills, experience, qualifications, and contact details from any resume format.
Can I import LinkedIn data automatically?+
