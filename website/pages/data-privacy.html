Frequently Asked Questions

What personal information does TalentPrism collect?+
For website visitors, we collect contact information when you request demos or support. For service users, we process candidate data including resumes, contact details, and employment history that you upload to our platform.
How is my data used and shared?+
Your data is used solely to provide our recruitment services, ensure platform security, and comply with legal obligations. We share data with trusted subprocessors for service delivery and may disclose information when required by law.
What security measures protect my data?+
We employ end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, access controls, and industry-standard security protocols. Our infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2 certified cloud platforms with 24/7 monitoring.
How long is data retained?+
Data is retained only as long as necessary for business purposes or legal requirements. You can request data deletion at any time, and we will comply within 30 days of your request.
What are my data protection rights?+
You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You can also request data portability and object to certain processing activities. Contact privacy@talentprism.com to exercise these rights.
Is my data transferred internationally?+
Yes, your data may be processed in countries with varying privacy laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards including standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions to protect your data during international transfers.
