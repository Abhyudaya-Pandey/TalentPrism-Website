Frequently Asked Questions

What metrics can I track with TalentPrism analytics?+
TalentPrism tracks over 50 key recruitment metrics including time-to-hire, cost-per-hire, application conversion rates, candidate quality scores, and team productivity measures.
Can I customize my analytics dashboard?+
Yes, TalentPrism offers fully customizable dashboards where you can add, remove, and rearrange widgets to focus on the metrics most relevant to your recruitment strategy.
How often is the analytics data updated?+
Analytics data is updated in real-time as actions occur in your recruitment pipeline, ensuring you always have the most current insights available.
Can I export reports for presentations?+
Absolutely. TalentPrism supports exporting reports in PDF, Excel, and PowerPoint formats, making it easy to share insights with stakeholders and executives.
