Frequently Asked Questions

Which metrics are included by default?+
TalentPrism ships with more than 50 recruiting KPIs covering sourcing, pipeline speed, recruiter productivity, and placement revenue—mirroring the breadth offered by Manatal’s analytics suite.
Can I build my own dashboards?+

How fresh is the data?+
Data refreshes in real-time as candidates move through your pipeline, so decisions are always based on the latest activity.

Export dashboards to PDF, CSV, or PowerPoint, or share secure links that always display the latest metrics.
