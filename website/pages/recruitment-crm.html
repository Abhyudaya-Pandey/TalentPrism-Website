Frequently Asked Questions

How does the recruitment CRM differ from traditional CRM systems?+
TalentPrism's recruitment CRM is specifically designed for talent acquisition, with features like candidate nurturing, referral tracking, and recruitment-specific analytics that traditional CRMs lack.
Can I import my existing candidate database?+
Yes, TalentPrism supports bulk import of candidate data from CSV files, existing ATS systems, and other CRM platforms to maintain continuity in your relationships.
How does the CRM help with GDPR compliance?+
The CRM includes consent management, data retention controls, and audit trails to ensure compliance with data protection regulations while maintaining candidate relationships.
Can I automate candidate communications?+
Absolutely. Set up automated email sequences for nurturing, follow-ups, and relationship building based on candidate actions, engagement levels, and timeline triggers.
