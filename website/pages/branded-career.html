Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up a branded career page?+
Most customers have their branded career page live within 24-48 hours. Our templates and drag-and-drop editor make customization quick and easy.
Can I use my own domain for the career page?+
Yes, you can use a custom subdomain like careers.yourcompany.com or integrate it seamlessly into your existing website.
Do I need design skills to create a professional-looking page?+
Not at all. Our intuitive editor and professionally designed templates ensure your career page looks polished and professional, regardless of your design experience.
How does the career page integrate with my existing ATS?+
TalentPrism's career pages sync automatically with your ATS, ensuring job listings are always up-to-date and applications flow seamlessly into your recruitment pipeline.
